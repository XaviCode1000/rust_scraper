# Getting started

Let's Encrypt is a Certificate Authority that provides free TLS certificates, making it easy for websites to enable HTTPS encryption and create a more secure Internet for everyone. Let's Encrypt is a project of the nonprofit Internet Security Research Group.

![](/images/le-logo-lockonly.svg) Ready to begin?

+



Start securing your websites with free SSL/TLS certificates from Let's Encrypt. In order to get a certificate for your website's domain, you have to demonstrate control over the domain.

[Let's Get Started →](/getting-started)

![](/images/le-logo-lockonly.svg) Documentation

+



Learn about our certificate issuance process and best practices.

[Read Documentation →](/docs)

![](/images/le-logo-lockonly.svg) ACME Clients

+



Choose from our recommended ACME clients to automatically manage your certificates.

[Browse ACME Clients →](/docs/client-options)

![](/images/le-logo-lockonly.svg) Get help from the Let's Encrypt Community

+



Join our community forum for technical assistance and knowledge sharing with experts, volunteers, and ISRG staff.

[Visit the Forum →](https://community.letsencrypt.org/)

## More ways we're improving security and privacy
